Highlights

For the past two years, I've been part of really exciting projects that have taken the daily activities of my clients to the next level while promoting sustainable practices.

üí® Award-winning Dust Control Solution:

The operations at industrial sites, such as steel manufacturers, often produce a large amount of dust, which reduces visibility and air quality on the site and in neighboring areas. The local authorities have noticed the dust generated at our client's site and have determined that operations will need to cease unless a solution for the dust is found.

  • Problem:

    • The logistics department on site used water tank trucks to water the entire area and reduce airborne dust. However, the dust levels remained high, and it was difficult to track the trucks' activities to determine better ways to act upon the issue.

  • Objective:

    • Provide visibility and control over the activities of trucks and their water usage.

  • Solution:

    • Mobile IoT application for water truck operators that detects and controls water usage through sensors plus a web dashboard that provides real-time information on the trucks' activities, location, and water usage. As a result of using this app, the company's water consumption has been reduced by 80%, leading to them receiving an award for innovation and environmental commitment.

üîß Fleet Maintenance Solution:

The maintenance and inspection of fleet assets are crucial to guarantee efficiency and continued operations.

  • Problem:

    • The client used to provide asset operators with paper pre-start checks that they needed to complete every day manually. The amount of paperwork generated made it really difficult to keep assets in working order. Many of them remained inactive or were used even if they were not up to it, which worsened the asset's conditions, impacted performance, and imposed risks on operators' safety.

  • Objective:

    • Digitalize the pre-start check process to guarantee the working state of the entire fleet and improve efficiency.

  • Solution:

    • Cross platform solution made of a mobile app that allows users to complete pre-start checks digitally and a web dashboard with real-time information highlighting urgent issues that require immediate attention. This allows for a quick overview of the fleet's condition at any given time. The web interface also enables mechanics and supervisors to remotely view pre-start checks, filter assets, and edit or upload check questionnaires. Overall, this solution aims to reduce asset downtime and ensure that assets are consistently maintained and up-to-date.
