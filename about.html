Hola! I’m Tamara Abeldaño, a UI/UX Designer with 1+ years experience at a Christchurch start-up.

In my current role, I’ve been crafting all kinds of solutions, from management apps for the mining and steel sector to a scooter mobile app and an asset tracking platform.

Before immersing myself in UX, I used to be a translator and my interest in communication and design finally led me to discover this broad and exciting field.

Ideation is my favourite part of the job but, as a generalist, I'm involved in every step of the design process, ensuring a smooth transition from design to development.

If you are reading this, rest assured my main goal is to blend functionality and beauty to land the best solutions our clients can get. :)
